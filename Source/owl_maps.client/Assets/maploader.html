Upload Custom Interior Mapping

In this window, you are able to upload a custom interior for your interior. The interior must be owned by yourself or from the faction you're a manager of. You can only upload your map in an XML way. More info on how to do this can be found on the CommunityName Forums.

An interior costs 500 GCs. Upon purchasing the custom interior, you'll have 24 hours to make changes to your custom interior if it's not up to your needs. After 24 hours, you'll have 1 week of reduced costs. The reduction is 250 GCs. After a week you'll be paying the regular amount.
